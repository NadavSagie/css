How I Work

My QA approach is based on understanding product intent, testing with structure, documenting clearly, and helping teams move toward stable, reliable releases.

My Testing Approach

  • Understand the feature, requirements, and expected behavior
  • Break the feature into positive, negative, and edge-case scenarios
  • Execute tests methodically while keeping the user experience in mind
  • Report bugs clearly with steps to reproduce and visible impact
  • Retest fixes and support regression cycles with consistency

My Path to QA

My path to QA started through hands-on work with operational systems during my military service. In a training and command environment, I collaborated around command-and-control platforms, participated in identifying issues, validating functionality, and giving structured feedback on software behavior.

Later, my academic and professional background in research strengthened my analytical thinking, communication skills, and attention to accuracy. Together, these experiences led me naturally to QA: a role that combines structure, investigation, clarity, and responsibility for quality.

I am especially interested in junior QA roles where I can contribute quickly, keep learning, and grow into a strong, reliable team member.
